* Easy access to processing of unstructured content with instant connection to ABBYY’s intelligent capture solutions
* Leverage leading OCR and Intelligent Document Processing technologies to automate document processing
* Automate the classification of documents and extraction of data, turning unstructured data into structured, actionable information
